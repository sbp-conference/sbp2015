Registration Information

BRiMS is co-located with SBP. Registration for SBP Conference covers entrance to BRiMS.

Early registration ends February 27, 2015 at 11:59 PM EST.

  • Early General Registration (price increases Feb 27) : $325.00
  • Early Student Registration (price increases Feb 27) : $275.00

Regular registration start Feb 27, 2015 at 12:00 AM EST.

  • General Registration: $460.00
  • Student Registration: $350.00

How to register:

Fill out the form at http://reg.conferences.dce.ufl.edu/Basic/1400042676​
